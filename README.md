# homeready
HomeReady - concept prototype
Mortgage Readiness App
A product concept and interactive prototype exploring how first home buyers could better understand their mortgage readiness before ever walking into a bank.
**The Problem**
The home buying journey in Australia is fragmented across brokers, lenders, government schemes and conveyancers, with no single touchpoint that helps renters understand where they actually stand.
**The Concept**
HomeReady gives users a personalised view of their mortgage readiness through a soft credit pull, financial profile assessment, and readiness tier — with gamified milestones to keep users engaged across a journey that can take years, not weeks.
Designed for both the Australian and US markets, with UX benchmarked against established home lending platforms including JP Morgan Chase.

Status: Concept / Prototype — not a live product.
